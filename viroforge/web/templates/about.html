About ViroForge

What is ViroForge?

ViroForge is a comprehensive mock metavirome data generator for benchmarking and validating virome analysis pipelines. It generates realistic synthetic FASTQ datasets with complete ground truth metadata.

Key Features
  • 28 Curated Collections - Host-associated, environmental, and disease state viromes
  • 14,423 Viral Genomes - Complete RefSeq database with ICTV taxonomy
  • 5 Sequencing Platforms - NovaSeq, MiSeq, HiSeq, PacBio HiFi, Oxford Nanopore
  • VLP Enrichment Modeling - 5 protocols with realistic contamination reduction
  • RNA Virome Workflow - RT, rRNA depletion, degradation modeling
  • Complete Ground Truth - Taxonomy, abundances, workflow statistics
